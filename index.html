Professional Summary

Hello,

I am Lucas Henrique de Barros, a specialist in Business Intelligence (BI) and data architecture, with extensive experience in data analysis and visualization for medium and large companies. Throughout my career, I have developed strategic solutions for process optimization, allowing a clearer and more detailed view of key performance indicators (KPIs) in various areas, including logistics, finance, and sales.

My background includes a specialization in Data Analysis and data privacy (LGPD). I am currently in the process of completing an advanced course in Data Architecture, focusing on machine learning techniques, predictive analysis, and big data.

With practical experience in tools like Power BI, QlikSense, Google Looker, and SQL Server, I have implemented dynamic dashboards that provide actionable insights in real-time. I also work on integrating complex systems and automating workflows with Python and SQL, which allows me to optimize operations and significantly reduce costs.

In addition, I am passionate about working with multidisciplinary teams and constantly collaborating with stakeholders, offering personalized consultancy to transform data into effective strategies and improve decision-making.

Currently, I work as a BI Analyst at a Transportation company, where I am responsible for implementing innovative BI solutions and transforming complex data into clear and actionable insights for senior management.
